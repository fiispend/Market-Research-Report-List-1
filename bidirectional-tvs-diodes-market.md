<p><p>The bidirectional TVS diodes market is primarily categorized into axial and radial types. Axial bidirectional TVS diodes feature leads extending from opposite ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in tight spaces, often used in consumer electronics and automotive applications. Radial bidirectional TVS diodes, on the other hand, have leads that extend from the bottom, facilitating easier placement on circuit boards, ideal for compact applications in telecommunications and industrial equipment. Both types effectively protect against voltage spikes.</p></p>
