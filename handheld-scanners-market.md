<p><p>The handheld scanners market can be categorized into several types based on connectivity options. Bluetooth scanners offer wireless convenience, connecting to devices without cables. USB scanners connect via USB ports, providing a reliable and fast data transfer option. Wireless scanners optimize mobility and ease of use, allowing users to operate without physical connections, ideal for various scanning applications.</p></p>
